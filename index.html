Our Story

Healing Hands, Non-Profit Organization

At Healing Hands, we believe that the health of our planet and the well-being of its people are deeply connected. Our journey began with a simple idea: small actions can create a lasting impact. With a focus on environmental restoration, we work hand in hand with communities to protect nature, promote sustainable practices, and revive ecosystems. Whether it’s planting trees, cleaning rivers, or raising awareness, we’re committed to healing our environment and fostering a world where nature and humanity thrive together.

Our Mission

To protect and restore the environment through community-driven initiatives, sustainable practices, and education, creating a healthier planet for future generations.

  • To save Earth
  • To build a sustainable future

Kiran and Moeeza

Co-Founding Partners

Healing Hands was founded by two friends, Kiran and Moeeza, who share a deep passion for protecting the environment and giving back to nature. United by a common vision, they set out to create a platform where communities could come together to take meaningful action in healing our planet. With a shared belief that small acts of care can lead to big change, they’re on a mission to inspire others to join hands in creating a sustainable and thriving world for future generations.
